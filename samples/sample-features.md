# Feature Ideas — Q3 Planning

We're a B2B SaaS project management tool with ~5,000 paying teams. Planning next quarter's roadmap. Here are the candidates:

## 1. AI Task Summarization
Automatically generate daily/weekly summaries of project activity using AI. Users currently spend 15-20 min writing status updates manually. Early survey: 68% said they'd use this daily.

## 2. Gantt Chart View
A classic timeline/Gantt view. One of our top-requested features for 3 years — 1,200+ upvotes on the feedback board. Enterprise prospects often cite its absence as a reason not to buy.

## 3. Slack Integration (Bi-directional)
Two-way sync: create tasks from Slack messages, get task updates in Slack. Competitors (Asana, Linear) already have this. We have a one-way webhook today.

## 4. Custom Fields
Let teams define their own metadata fields on tasks (dropdowns, numbers, text, dates). Required by ~30% of enterprise deals currently in pipeline, blocking ~$800k ARR.

## 5. Mobile App (iOS + Android)
Native mobile app. Currently web-only with a responsive layout. Engineering estimate: 6 months for a two-person team.

## 6. Dark Mode
Users have asked for it. Low engineering effort — mostly CSS variables. Not tied to any business outcome we can measure.

## 7. Time Tracking
Log hours against tasks. Needed for agencies and consulting firms billing by the hour. Small segment (~8% of current users), but high NPS in that group.

## 8. Guest / External Collaborator Access
Allow non-paying users to view and comment on specific projects. Removes friction for sharing with clients. Legal needs to review data access implications.

## 9. Zapier / Make Integration
Connect to 5,000+ apps via Zapier and Make. Low engineering effort using their existing platforms. Reduces pressure to build one-off integrations.

## 10. Recurring Tasks
Tasks that automatically re-create on a schedule (daily standups, weekly reviews, monthly reports). Frequently requested by operations teams.
